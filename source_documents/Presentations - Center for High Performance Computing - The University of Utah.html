Training

CHPC has developed a series of courses to help users make the most of CHPC resources. We continue to add to and improve this series and present it every fall semester. During spring and summer semesters we present an abbreviated series of presentations. We welcome suggestions for additional presentations. 

The training sessions are available via zoom.  The zoom link is on the schedule linked below.

There is no cost associated with these training sessions.  Also, there is no need to register, with the exception of the  HPC Monthly Workshops - for these you must register with the workshop organizers.  See link below for additional details of the presentations.

Schedule of Fall 2024 presentations
